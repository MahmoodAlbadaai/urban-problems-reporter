How It Works

Follow these three simple steps to report an issue and help your city improve:

Step 1: Report

Provide details about the issue, including its type, location, and a brief description.

Step 2: Review

Our team reviews the issue and forwards it to the relevant authorities.

Step 3: Resolve

The authorities take action, and the issue is resolved. You can track the progress on our platform.
