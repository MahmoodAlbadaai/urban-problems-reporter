Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I report an issue?

Simply go to the "Report an Issue" page, fill out the form, and submit your report.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report potholes, broken streetlights, pollution, garbage dumps, water leaks, and more.

How long does it take to resolve an issue?

Resolution times depend on the severity of the issue and city resources. You can check the "Reported Issues" page for updates.
